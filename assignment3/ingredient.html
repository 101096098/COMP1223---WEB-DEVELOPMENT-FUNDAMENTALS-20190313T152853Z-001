Ingredients

  1. 2 cups of wheat flour
  2. 2 eggs
  3. 1 tablespoon of Fresh biological yeast
  4. 1 tablespoon butter
  5. 1 tablespoon of baking dessert
  6. Salt
  7. Milk to knead

Pizza Dough

Step 01

  1. In a ball melt the vegetable lard with warm water.
  2. You will get a liquid doughy.



Step 02

  1. Add the butter, the eggs mixing it well.
  2. Add the salt and the powder.
  3. Add the flour and start to knead the dough.
  4. Knead your dough until it stop getting stuck in your hands.

Usually it is necessary to let the dough rest for 30 minutes, for this dough you do not have to wait, you can stretch the dough immediately.


Step 03

  1. Divide your dough in portions, around 200g each one.
  2. Open the dough. You can open it with your hand or using a rolling pin.
  3. Create a disco with the dough.
  4. Grease a form with butter, and put the(disco)doug on it.
  5. Pre-heat the oven.
  6. Baking to 220C.
