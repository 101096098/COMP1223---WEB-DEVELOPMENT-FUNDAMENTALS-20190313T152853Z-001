Baking Desert Pizza

You can surprise with a Desert Pizza.

Nutella and Strawberry

  1. 5 tbsp of Nutella
  2. 1 punnet of strawberries
  3. 1 tbsp icing sugar
  4. 1 tbsp butter or olive oi
  5. Note: tbsp - table spoon

How to do it:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 220Â°C.
  2. Brush a pizza tray with olive oil.
  3. Make one quantity of pizza dough. Roll out to fit a tray and prick surface all over with a fork.
  4. Brush with olive oil or butter and bake for approx.10 mins or until golden.
  5. While warm spread with Nutella generously and top with fresh strawberries.
  6. Sprinkle sifted icing sugar of the top and slice while warm.

Banana Pizza

  1. 1/4 stick (2 tbs) unsalted butter butter
  2. 1/4 cup sugar
  3. 3 bananas
  4. 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  5. 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  6. 1-2 tsp milk
  7. 1/4 tsp vanilla
  8. Note: tbsp - table spoon

How to do it:

  1. Cut up three bananas in thick rounds and spread them over the dough.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the sugar, brown sugar and butter.
  3. Using a fork to combine the ingredients until the butter is chopped up and pea sized.
  4. Sprinkle over the top of the bananas. It will melt and cover the bananas perfectly. Add the cinnamon and blend again.
  5. Bake the pizza. When the sugar is melted and the crust is golden brown it is perfectly cooked.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes.
  7. While the pizza is cooling mix up the icing by combining the powdered sugar, milk and vanilla.
  8. The amount of milk will vary but make sure your icing is rather thick as it will melt under the heat of the pizza.
  9. Drizzle the icing over the pizza and serve
