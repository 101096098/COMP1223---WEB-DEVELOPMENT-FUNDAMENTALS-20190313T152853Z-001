Tips

These tips will help you do a delicious pizza any time you want!

  1. Make the dough and freeze in individual packages and than when you feel like eating a pizza, do it.
  2. Use freezer bags by placing a ball of 200g of dough for each medium pizza.
  3. It's a good way to save money on making pasta every time you want to eat homemade pizza.!
