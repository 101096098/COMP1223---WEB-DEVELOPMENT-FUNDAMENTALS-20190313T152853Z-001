Baking Pizza

Now is the funniest part.

Pick tour toppings and bake your pizza


Margherita Pizza

  1. 3 tablespoons tomato sauce (see tips)
  2. Extra-virgin olive oil
  3. 2 ¾ ounces fresh mozzarella
  4. 4 to 5 basil leaves, roughly torn
  5. Note: Don’t bake the basil leaves, put it when the pizza is already baked.


Pepperoni-Mushroom Make New York-Style Pizza

  1. Drizzle with olive oil, then top with 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  2. 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  3. Sautéed mushrooms
  4. Sliced pepperoni before baking
  5. Note: Don’t bake the basil leaves, put it when the pizza is already baked.


Eggplant Pizza

  1. Tomato sauce
  2. 1 large eggplant (1 1/4 pound)
  3. 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  4. About 3 tablespoons olive oil


Four Cheese Pizza

  1. Tomato sauce
  2. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  3. Mozzarella
  4. Parmesan
  5. Brie, cut or ripped into chunks
  6. Ricotta
